Bucket list

Safari Guide

Kruger Area
The gold standard. Fly into Skukuza, Nelspruit, or Hoedspruit. A guided lodge is worth every cent — rangers know exactly where to find the animals. Check whether game drives are included when booking.
Recommended: Experience Africa — experienceafrica.co.za — great for custom packages with drives included.
Pilanesberg (great alternative)
Big Five, malaria-free, just 3 hours from Jo'burg by car. No internal flight needed — great if you're short on time or budget.
🌿 Safari tipping: R100–250 (€5–12) per couple per night for your ranger and tracker is customary and greatly appreciated.
Explore the region

Western Cape Region

Wine Farms

Franschhoek
The "French Corner" — tiny, beautiful, and home to the Franschhoek Wine Tram, a hop-on-hop-off service to different estates. A perfect full day without needing to drive.
Estates: Haute Cabrière · Vrede en Lust · La Motte · Rickety Bridge · Boschendal · Zorgvliet
Stellenbosch
A vibrant university town with oak-lined streets and world-class estates. Great for a night out or a morning walk through the botanical gardens.
Estates: Waterford (chocolate & wine) · Warwick (David's favourite!) · Tokara (great restaurant) · Delaire Graff (luxurious) · Thelema · Spier · Babylonstoren (book Babel for lunch)
Constantia (Cape Town)
A half-day wine visit without leaving the city. Groot Constantia (historic, great food), Klein Constantia (famous dessert wine), Beau Constantia (spectacular views + Chefs Warehouse).
Paarl — near the venue
While you're here for the wedding — Spice Route Destination or Fairview: chocolate, beer, cheese, and wine all in one spot.
Somerset West
Meerlust (one of SA's best reds) · Vergelegen (beautiful grounds) · Lourensford (coffee roastery, multiple restaurants, lovely wines).

Cape Town

Beaches
Scenic, colder (Atlantic side): Clifton, Camps Bay, Hout Bay
Warmer water (False Bay): Muizenberg, Strand, Fish Hoek
Must-Dos
  • ⛰️Table Mountain — hike or cable car. Check wind; it closes when too windy.
  • 🌅Lion's Head — sunset hike, ~1 hour up. Bring a headlamp for the descent!
  • ⚓Robben Island — historic and moving. Worth the boat trip.
  • 🎨Bo Kaap — colourful neighbourhood, Cape Malay food. Try Faeeza's Home Kitchen.
  • 🦭The Waterfront — markets, restaurants, seals, a great afternoon.
Day Trips
  • 🐧Simonstown / Kalk Bay (45 min) — penguins at Boulders Beach, charming harbour, Kalkys fish & chips.
  • 🐋Hermanus (2 hrs) — whale watching. October is a great time for sightings.
  • 🪁Langebaan / Paternoster (2 hrs) — kite surfing, incredible seafood, beautiful west coast.

Food & Drink

South African Foods to Try
  • 🥩Biltong & droëwors — dried cured meat, at any petrol station. Far better than jerky.
  • 🌭Boerewors roll — grilled sausage in a roll. Classic SA street food.
  • 🍛Bobotie & bunny chow — spiced mince bake and a bread bowl of curry.
  • 🍮Milk tart, malva pudding, koeksisters — order any of these whenever you see them.
  • 🥂MCC (Méthode Cap Classique) — SA's answer to Champagne. JC le Roux does a lovely nougat pairing.
  • 🍹Amarula — creamy marula fruit liqueur. Over ice.
