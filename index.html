FAQs

Can I bring a plus one?
Due to space, we're only able to accommodate the guests named on your invitation.
Are children welcome at the wedding?
The ceremony and reception are adults-only. Children are warmly welcome at the pre-wedding lunch (28th) and farewell picnic (30th). We recommend Wineland Nannies for childcare at your accommodation.
Is there a shuttle to the venue?
Yes — a shuttle will be provided for guests staying in Paarl on the wedding night. Please let us know your accommodation so we can add you to the pick-up list.
Do I need a visa for South Africa?
EU, UK, USA and Swedish passport holders are visa-exempt for up to 90 days. Check the South African Home Affairs website if you're unsure about your nationality.

🍷 Wine Farms

Franschhoek
The "French Corner" — home to the Franschhoek Wine Tram, a hop-on-hop-off service to different estates. A perfect full day without needing to drive.
Estates: Haute Cabrière · Vrede en Lust · La Motte · Rickety Bridge · Boschendal · Zorgvliet
Stellenbosch
A vibrant university town with oak-lined streets and world-class estates.
Estates: Waterford · Warwick (David's favourite!) · Tokara · Delaire Graff · Babylonstoren
Constantia
A half-day wine visit without leaving Cape Town. Groot Constantia, Klein Constantia, Beau Constantia.
Paarl — near the venue
Spice Route Destination or Fairview: chocolate, beer, cheese, and wine all in one spot.

🌊 Cape Town

  • ⛰️Table Mountain — hike or cable car. Check wind forecasts; it closes when too windy.
  • 🌅Lion's Head — sunset hike, ~1 hour up. Bring a headlamp for the descent!
  • ⚓Robben Island — historic and moving. Worth the boat trip.
  • 🎨Bo Kaap — colourful neighbourhood, Cape Malay food.
  • 🏖️Beaches — Clifton & Camps Bay (scenic, cooler) · Muizenberg & Strand (warmer water)

🚗 Day Trips

  • 🐧Simonstown / Kalk Bay (45 min) — penguins at Boulders Beach, charming harbour, Kalkys fish & chips.
  • 🐋Hermanus (2 hrs) — whale watching. October is peak season for sightings.
  • 🪁Langebaan / Paternoster (2 hrs) — kite surfing, incredible seafood, beautiful west coast.

🍴 SA Foods to Try

  • 🥩Biltong & droëwors — dried cured meat at any petrol station. Far better than jerky.
  • 🌭Boerewors roll — grilled sausage in a roll. Classic SA street food.
  • 🍛Bobotie & bunny chow — spiced mince bake and a bread bowl of curry.
  • 🍮Milk tart, malva pudding, koeksisters — order any of these whenever you see them.
  • 🥂MCC (Méthode Cap Classique) — SA's answer to Champagne.
